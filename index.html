Bone Appetit

There is a mystery to be solve!

The dog bones in Pete's treat jar has suspiciously disappeared.

The trail of crumbs has led Pete to his four dogs, and they sure look guilty.

Help Pete find all the treats from each dog.


DIRECTIONS:

  • By clicking on a dog, you will add a specific amount of bones to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number of bones needed to be collect.
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number of bones.
  • The value of each bones from the dog is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, it will change the values of each bones Pete need.
